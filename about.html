Who we are

Gamafour Integrated Systems is the brand division of EDGA Atlantic Solutions Ltd. EDGA birthed Gamafour to deliver integrated solutions that connect risk control, compliance, and performance across HSE, Environmental, and ESG requirements.

Our focus is not paperwork for its own sake — it’s controls, evidence, and implementation that hold up when clients, regulators, or auditors ask hard questions.

What “Integrated Systems” means

One coordinated management approach — not separate documents fighting each other. We align policies, procedures, registers, training, audits, and reporting into a single operating system.

  • Risk-based operational controls
  • Measurement, reporting, and close-out discipline
